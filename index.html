Our Flagship Asset

Former Shangri‑La Vancouver

The iconic mixed-use skyscraper at 1128 West Georgia Street was acquired by ET Investment Group on June 14, 2025.

  • 50‑storey luxury hotel & residences
  • Located in downtown Vancouver
  • Includes spa, Urban Fare, gallery, sculpture garden

Rebrand to Park Hyatt

After the CAD 183 million purchase, the hotel will relaunch under the prestigious Park Hyatt brand on June 30, 2025.

This marks North America’s first Park Hyatt in a mixed-use tower.

Preserving Heritage

A restored 1919-era Coastal Church on-site received a CAD 4.4 million heritage renovation as part of the deal.
